Fight Songs: From Belonging to Battle

College fight songs are among the oldest shared rituals in American athletics. While often treated as lighthearted traditions, their lyrics encode how institutions imagine identity, competition, and success. This project asks whether fight songs have shifted over time from emphasizing internal belonging toward defining victory through the defeat of opponents.
